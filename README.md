# moodbuddies-ux-case-study

## Overview
MoodBuddies is a mental wellness mobile app designed to help users track their mood, feel emotionally supported, and connect with others in a safe, low-pressure way.

## Problem
Many people struggle to express how they feel or feel isolated when dealing with emotional stress. Existing mental health apps often feel overwhelming, clinical, or impersonal.

## Goal
Design a calm, supportive experience that encourages emotional check-ins without pressure or judgment.

## Research
- Identified emotional friction in traditional mental health apps
- Focused on simplicity, privacy, and emotional safety
- Prioritized minimal steps and gentle prompts

## User Flow
1. Open app
2. Quick mood check-in
3. Optional reflection or connection
4. Gentle encouragement and resources

## Wireframes
Low-fidelity wireframes focused on reducing cognitive load and emotional overwhelm.

## Visual Design
- Soft color palette to reduce anxiety
- Rounded elements for warmth
- Minimal copy and gentle language

## Tools Used
- Figma
- Coursera UX frameworks

## Outcome
MoodBuddies demonstrates a user-centered approach to designing emotionally supportive digital experiences.
